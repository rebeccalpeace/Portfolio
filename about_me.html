background

I was born in Lake Jackson, TX and raised in Austin, TX for most of my life. I attended UT Austin and received a double major in Studio Art and Geography. After college, I obtained a certification to teach in Texas and taught elementary music for three years. As exciting as this was, it wasn't the right career choice for me, so I went back to school to get a nursing degree from UTMB. During these last four years, I have been nursing in different hospital units, from med/surg to labor and delivery. I am currently living in Philadelphia, PA with my boyfriend and beloved cat, Peepee.

why web dev

After being an RN for four years, I've had enough experience to know that I am not passionate about nursing. I took a beginning web development course at NuCamp in May 2022 and I really enjoyed what I learned about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am excited about what a career in web development has to offer: creativity, flexibility, camaraderie, and a challenging, yet fulfilling experience.
